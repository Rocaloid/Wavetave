Plugin_F0Marking

Finds the fundamental frequency by looking for peaks in magnitude spectrum.

Stores the fundamental frequency in global variable Plugin_Var_F0(in bin-index form).

Algorithm

From left(low-freq) to right(high-freq) in the decibel magnitude spectrum, find the spectral peak which has the biggest difference to the maximum of spectrum on its left.
